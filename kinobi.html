Kinobi

Identity Creation, Stationery, Web Design & Development, UI Design  |  Client: Kinobi Co

Kinobi is an online platform where people learn from instructional videos. What sets Kinobi apart from traditional video websites is a split screen feature where the users' webcam acts as a second video feed so they can see how well they're following the instructor video.

As the lead designer for Kinobi and founding team member, responsibilities included brand creation, development and implementation, web design and UI/UX development, and promotion materials for print and video.

Kinobi is a tech start-up recently graduated from the New Orleans Launchpad accelerator program and has exhibited on stage at Tech Crunch Disrupt in New York.
