20th Century | History of Graphic Design

Print Poster Series

My love of graphic design history (in particular the 20th century) compelled me to design this series of posters - essentially a timeline of 20th century graphic design. I created 5 main categories; Technological Advancements, World Events, Graphic Design Pioneers, Graphic Design Milestones, and Art Movements. These categories were then color coded to help define the who-what-where-when nature of historical timelines.
