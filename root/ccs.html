The Center for Campus Sustainability (CCS)

Identity Creation, Promotional Brochure, Event Banners, Web-based Annual Report  |  Client: Center for Campus Sustainability (CCS)

The Center for Campus Sustainability (CCS) is a new department within the office of Facility Services at LSU. I was employed by the CCS to design their identity and graphic communications. This required a logo, color scheme, and visual marks for the four sub-departments of the CCS - Energy, Facility, Materials and Process. Promotional materials included a Year at a Glance trifold brochure, and large scale banners for use at conventions and site-specific events.

The CCS annual report was designed as an interactive publication to be viewed online, giving the reader the opportunity to print the report if desired. The publication is a full color, 8.5" x 11", 60 page report. Use the arrows at the foot of the document for navigation. Roll over the Contents footer to access the table of contents on any page.
